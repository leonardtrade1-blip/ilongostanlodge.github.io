About Us

14 Years of Luxury Hospitality

Since 2012, ILONGOSTAN LODGE has been Tanzania's premier hospitality destination, offering an extraordinary blend of modern luxury and affordable excellence. With three strategically located branches — in Dodoma, Mbeya, and Dar es Salaam — we provide travelers and business guests with world-class comfort and impeccable service.

Our focus is on delivering a luxury experience at an affordable price. Every detail has been thoughtfully crafted — from architecturally stunning buildings to meticulously furnished rooms featuring premium bedding and elegant stone finishes.
