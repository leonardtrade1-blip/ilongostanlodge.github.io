About Us

A Legacy of Luxury Hospitality

ILONGOSTAN LODGE is Tanzania's premier hospitality destination, offering an extraordinary blend of modern luxury and authentic African warmth. With three strategically located branches across the nation — in Dodoma, Mbeya, and Dar es Salaam — we provide travelers and business guests with world-class comfort, impeccable service, and an experience that transcends the ordinary.

Every detail at ILONGOSTAN LODGE has been thoughtfully crafted — from our architecturally stunning buildings adorned with ambient lighting, to our meticulously furnished rooms featuring premium bedding, modern climate control, and elegant stone-finished bathrooms.
